About

I am a Data Scientist with an engineer's mindset for problem-solving. My foundation in Mechanical Engineering provided me with a rigorous training in analytical thinking, mathematical modeling, and systems analysis. During my studies, I discovered my true passion lies not in designing physical systems, but in architecting intelligent systems from data. Following this passion, I dedicated the past year to an intensive, self-driven curriculum focused on the full data science lifecycle. This portfolio is the result: a collection of over 20 end-to-end projects demonstrating my ability to move fluidly from deep-dive SQL analytics and Python scripting to building and deploying production-grade machine learning models. I am now eager to apply my unique blend of engineering discipline and hands-on data expertise to solve complex business challenges.
